## Executive Summary
JD.com's next major strategic expansion targets are food delivery and instant retail, explicitly identified by the CEO as long-term strategic directions, alongside international business and innovative catering models like Seven Fresh Kitchen. This strategic pivot is validated by explosive growth in the company's 'new business' segment, which saw revenue surge by 157.3% in 2025. While the exact unit economics and capital allocation for these new ventures remain opaque, the aggressive market entry and executive guidance confirm that on-demand local commerce and international expansion are JD's primary growth engines beyond its core e-commerce operations.

Executive leadership has explicitly defined food delivery and instant retail as core long-term strategies. JD officially entered the food delivery market in early 2025, signaling a major shift toward on-demand, localized commerce.

Beyond standard delivery, JD is innovating with models like Seven Fresh Kitchen to capture the catering market, while simultaneously pushing international business as a key innovation pillar.
